Application

Apply at this link.

Applications received by April 15th receive priority consideration. Applicants will hear back by May 15th.

DHRI @ SMU is based on the DHRI as developed at Graduate Center Digital Initiatives at The Graduate Center, CUNY.

Read more about our philosophy here.


DHRI participants are expected to commit to the full 4 ­day experience from August 19-­22, 2019 and to attend all of the workshops and events during this time. By submitting an application to DHRI, participants acknowledge and accept the above expectations.

As this is a pilot program focused on building capacity as a community, we will have limited seats. (We expect the majority of particpants to be part of the SMU community, but do have a limited number of seats for participants outside of SMU).

The application contains sections designed to help us get a better sense of the kind of digital projects you might be interested in working on. You will have to answer the following questions:

* Please share information about your goals, interests, and needs.

*Please describe your research interests.

*How will your participation in DHRI benefit your personal research and professional or pedagogical goals?

*Describe an instance when you ran into a technical problem that you didn't know the answer to right away. How did you overcome it?

*What type of role do you feel most comfortable taking on when working in a group? Describe a time you worked on a group project; what role did you take on and how would you describe your contribution to the group?

Questions and comments can be directed to Rafia Mirza.
